Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

PeptideBro ("the app") is a personal tracking journal. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. We do not operate servers that receive your data.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter into PeptideBro — doses, stack items, personal notes, settings — is stored only on your device, inside Apple's sandboxed Application Support directory for the app. Uninstalling the app removes all of it.

Third parties

The app does not include third-party analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or tracking SDKs. It does not share data with third parties because it has no data to share.

Network

The app does not make network requests during normal use. It may make a request if you tap an external link (for example, this page or the Terms of Use); in that case your device's browser, not the app, handles the request.

Children

The app is intended for adults aged 18 and over. It is not directed to children. A first-launch acknowledgement requires the user to confirm they are 18 or older.

Changes

If we ever change this policy, the "Last updated" date above will change. Substantive changes will be surfaced in-app.

Contact

Questions: support@peptidebro.app
